Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is FootfallCam?

FootfallCam is an AI-powered people counting and visitor analytics solution that helps businesses understand foot traffic and customer behavior.

It provides accurate insights such as visitor count, dwell time, and movement patterns using 3D stereo vision technology.

Q2: Which industries use FootfallCam solutions?

FootfallCam is widely used across industries including retail, shopping malls, offices, airports, museums, universities, and smart city projects.

These industries rely on FootfallCam to improve operational efficiency, space planning, and customer experience.

Q3: How does FootfallCam count visitors accurately?

FootfallCam uses 3D stereo vision cameras combined with AI algorithms to detect and track human movement.

This allows the system to filter out non-human objects and maintain high accuracy even in crowded environments.
