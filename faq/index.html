Frequently Asked Questions

What is FootfallCam?

FootfallCam is an AI-powered people counting and visitor analytics solution that helps businesses understand foot traffic and customer behavior.

Which industries use FootfallCam solutions?

FootfallCam is widely used in retail, shopping malls, offices, airports, museums, universities, and smart city projects.

How does FootfallCam count visitors accurately?

FootfallCam uses 3D stereo vision and AI algorithms to accurately count people while filtering out non-human objects.
